My name is Zhewei Hu. I am {{ myage }} years old. I am a second-year master student majoring in Computer Science at NC State University. I was born and grew up in Cixi, the city below Shanghai.

I started to "play" with Ruby and Rails in Nov. 2014, so it is more than one year now. Since that time, I began to work on an open source project named Expertiza.

Expertiza is a web application supported by NSF written by Ruby where students can submit and peer-review learning objects. I contributed more than 350 commits to Expertiza master branch on GitHub. And I designed and deployed several key features, such as multi-part rubrics, team lazy initialization, automatic review mapping strategy, student suggested topics, etc.

I am also familiar with RSpec testing framework. Recently, I refactored and merged several pull requests to increase the test coverage from 28% to 36%.

This semester, I am a research assistant of Expertiza group. My job focuses on maintain the system and do research related to education data mining. I have already jointly published 2 papers.
